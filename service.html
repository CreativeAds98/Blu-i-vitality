Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I know which therapy is right for me?
Our practitioners will conduct a comprehensive health assessment during your first consultation and recommend a personalised care pathway based on your goals and medical history.
Are the therapies safe?
All our therapies are administered by trained professionals using medical-grade equipment and sterile protocols. We follow safety guidelines and conduct pre-screening for every patient.
How many sessions will I need?
This varies by therapy and individual. Some patients experience benefits after one session, while others benefit from a structured course. We will create a personalised plan for you.
Can therapies be combined?
Yes. Many patients follow integrative protocols combining multiple therapies for enhanced results — for example, IV nutrition alongside functional medicine consultations.
What should I do to prepare for a session?
Stay hydrated, eat a light meal beforehand, and wear comfortable clothing. Specific instructions will be given when you book.
Do you offer membership packages?
Yes — we offer membership plans with discounted access to multiple therapies, priority booking, and ongoing wellness support. Ask us about current packages.
